Architecture

Advanced Detection Engine

Understanding how TruthGuard processes raw inputs into deterministic integrity scores.

Machine Learning Based Detection

Leverages a trained Logistic Regression classifier optimized with L2 regularization to balance high accuracy with generalization capability.

TF-IDF Feature Extraction

Constructs vectors of Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency using unigrams and bigrams, picking up on stylistic markers and content cues.

Natural Language Processing

Applies standard text cleaning routines, eliminates high-frequency English stop words, and processes raw content systematically.

Real-Time Analysis

Operates entirely in-memory to classify paragraphs and headlines instantly, presenting direct probability metrics within milliseconds.

Model Specifications

TruthGuard Lite runs on a pipeline combining supervised vectorization and linear classification. It is trained on the ISOT Fake and Real news collection to distinguish between highly structured true news wires and untrustworthy blogs/memes.
